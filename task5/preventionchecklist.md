# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. You should use unique passwords for all of your accounts and enable two-factor authentication.

2. Never share one time passwords or passwords or bank details with anyone even if they claim to be from your bank.

3. Do not click on links that seem download attachments from people you do not know.

4. Always check the website address before you enter login information.

5. Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security problems.

6. Be careful when you use Wi-Fi and try to avoid logging into sensitive accounts on shared networks.

7. Review and adjust your privacy settings on your media accounts regularly.

8. Do not share information like your phone number or address or college details publicly.

### UPI / Financial Safety Tips

9. Never approve collect requests in UPI apps unless you are sure you are paying someone you know.

10. Remember that receiving money does not require you to enter a PIN or approve a request in UPI apps.

11. Only use banking and payment apps that you download from trusted app stores, like the official app store.
